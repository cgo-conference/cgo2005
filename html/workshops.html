CGO 2005 Workshops and Tutorials

This year at CGO 2005 we are planning 1 day of pre-conference workshops and tutorials.

The following workshops are being planned:

    ODES: Workshop on Optimizations for DSP and Embedded Systems

    MRE05: Workshop on Managed Runtime Environments

    EPIC: Workshop on Explicitly Parallel Instruction Computing Techniques

The following tutorial is being planned:

     Tutorial: Software dynamic translation: Challenges, approaches and applications
     Presenters: Bruce Childers (University of Pittsburgh), Jack Davidson (University of Virginia)
